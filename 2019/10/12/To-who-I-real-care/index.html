To who I real care

Oct 12, 2019 | Hits
Comment

We didn‘t know much about each other 7 years ago, and unfortunately now it seems to be still the case. We were in the same class for two years and these two years surely, was the most important time in both of our lives. I remember the first time I saw you was through the window of the classroom when I get past it and I did remember that you saw(glance at) me at that time too. I don’t know why this scenery leaves such a deep impression since the facts that I didn’t have any feelings about you even several months after the moment. Actually, I’ve forgotten most things happened in that classroom. But, amazingly, unbelievably, and all of a sudden, I found I’ve trapped all of my mind on you and you did swept me off my feet every times I saw you. Later, When I knew you failed the college entrance examination(me too) and would return to XiuYuan for another year(me too), I was praying to God hoping he/she gives me the chance that we could be allocated to the same class again. And we all know the result - God did satisfied me. And thanks to God, I could saw you every day in the classroom. And it’s you, in those darkest days, brings me the hope, light up my sky and give me the passion and belief that I must work hard to make a better future and make me powerful enough to give you something special. Slowly, gradually, I found the feeling is not only about like; It’s about love, responsibility. I was always guided by my intuition — and years passed, the intuition keeps telling me you will be and should be the one for me cause No Reason. You’re still the apple of my eyes, and will be forever, no matter what you said or did, and in which way you’d like to treat me or think of me and how you have made me sad or frustrated in these past several batshit days. 5 years passed, good times, hard times, we didn’t share with each other; now we’re still here. One more 5 years later, the world will be totally changed. All of the old classmates will graduate, get married, begin to have and educate their own kids like what their parents have done with them. And at that time, where would we be?
